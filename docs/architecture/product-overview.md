# TinyScholar Product Architecture

## Purpose of this document

This document records the product-oriented architecture for TinyScholar and the technology decisions that guide its incremental implementation. It is a living document and must be updated when those decisions change.

## Product overview

TinyScholar is a learning platform for children aged 6 and above. Parents manage a family workspace, its children, learning books and materials, and learning progress. Each child has an independent login account and educational profile, so a child can study without an active parent session. Multiple children in the same family can study simultaneously on separate devices.

The platform will provide trustworthy, child-appropriate learning experiences, including material-based retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), learning activities, and progress tracking.
